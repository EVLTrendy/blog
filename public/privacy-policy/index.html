Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-05

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name and email address when you subscribe or contact us.
  • Usage Data: anonymised analytics such as page views and referring URLs.
  • Cookies: small files stored on your device to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the blog.
  • To send newsletters you have opted into.
  • To analyse site performance and improve content.
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Analytics: For traffic measurement and site analytics.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Netlify: For website hosting and content delivery.

These third parties may collect data as described in their own privacy policies. You can visit Google's Privacy Policy to learn more about how Google uses data.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to serve relevant advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to display personalized ads based on your interests. You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings
  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' default retention periods.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@evolvedlotus.com.
