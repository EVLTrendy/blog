# Research

## Page: /admin/#/collections/blog (Blog Posts)

### Feature: Blog Post Editor
#### What I Saw (Visual Description)
- A comprehensive form with a split-pane view (editor left, preview right).
- **Basic Information**: Title (text), Body (markdown rich text), Categories (dropdown), Tags (list widget), Content Hubs (relation), Featured (toggle).
- **Media**: Featured Image (image upload), Alt Text (text).
- **Organization & Tags**: Categories and Tags fields are prominent.
- **Editorial Workflow**: Status (dropdown: Draft, In Review, etc.), Dates (Publish, Scheduled, Last Updated, Next Review), Notes (textarea).
- **SEO Optimization**: Focus Keyword, Search Intent (dropdown), Meta Title, Meta Description, Canonical URL.
- **Content Strategy**: Type (dropdown), Evergreen/Seasonal (toggles), Priority (dropdown).
- **Content Series**: Series Name, Episode Number.
- **Content Repurposing**: URLs for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter, Podcast, LinkedIn; Email Newsletter toggle.
- **Performance Metrics**: Score, Page Views, Time on Page, Bounce Rate, Conversion Rate (number fields).

#### What I Did (Actions Taken)
- Navigated to the Blog collection.
- Clicked "New Blog Post".
- Expanded all collapsible groups (Basic Info, Media, SEO, Editorial, Strategy, Series, Repurposing, Performance).
- Scrolled through the entire form.

#### What Happened (Actual Behavior)
- The form loaded correctly.
- All collapsible sections expanded smoothly.
- Fields were present and accessible.
- The split-pane preview updated (or was ready to update) as fields were viewed.

#### What I Expected (Intended Purpose)
- To see all the fields required for the "Core Content Management" of blog posts as specified.

#### Bugs / Missing Features / Errors
- None observed. All specified fields for Blog Posts appear to be present.
