<h2>The Dark Side: What Creators Need to Watch Out For</h2>
<h3>Hidden Costs of &quot;Free&quot; Tools</h3>
<p>Free plans often restrict exports, add watermarks, limit quality to 720p, and lock essential features behind paywalls. A &quot;free&quot; tool charging $5 per export becomes $100/month for 20 client videos.</p>
<h3>Billing Nightmares</h3>
<p>Some platforms make cancellation difficult or add surprise charges for features that seemed included. Always review what's included at your chosen tier and confirm cancellation policies.</p>
<h3>The &quot;AI Slop&quot; Problem</h3>
<p>Low-quality, mass-produced AI content floods platforms without real thought or storytelling. Algorithms detect patterns of low-effort content and reduce visibility. Balance AI technical tasks with human creative control.</p>
<h3>Technical Limitations</h3>
<p>AI struggles with humor timing, subtle sarcasm, and emotional nuance. Always review AI outputs before publishing. AI is a brilliant assistant, not a fully independent editor.</p>
